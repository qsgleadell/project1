Background

Quinn's love for computers and programming started in high school, where he built his own PC and gained over 3000 hours of coding experience. He was also an artist, competing in multiple art competitions.

At university, Quinn wished to combine his passion for art and computer science, but he eventually fell in love with cybersecurity. He is a member of the Cybersecurity club and enjoys participating in CTFs.

Quinn previously worked as a Swim Instructor at Emler Swim School in Houston for almost two years. He learned the importance of teamwork and was able to make a lasting impact on children and adults through his work.

Goals

Quinn's goal is to use his passion for cybersecurity and teamwork to make a lasting impact in his future career.
